About

Originally trained as an economist, I now use a wide array of methodological tools for research including experiments, surveys, LLMs, AI, and ML. I am currently preparing for a research visit to rural Laos to analyze social networks.

My research primarily focuses on the interaction of humans with AI and the self-regulation of groups in social networks.

Final stages of my PhD in Behavioural Economics at Heidelberg University.
